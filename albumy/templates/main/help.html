Frequently Asked Questions

Storage Produce Guidelines

Here are some frequently asked questions about storing produce:

  • Q: How should I store leafy greens?
    Leafy greens like lettuce and spinach should be stored in a plastic bag in the crisper drawer of your refrigerator.
  • Q: What's the best way to store root vegetables?
    Root vegetables like potatoes and carrots should be stored in a cool, dark place, away from onions and garlic.

Refrigerator Produce Guidelines

Here are some frequently asked questions about refrigerator produce:

  • Q: Can I refrigerate tomatoes?
    Tomatoes are best stored at room temperature to maintain their flavor and texture.
  • Q: How long can I keep fruits like strawberries in the fridge?
    Fresh berries usually last 3-5 days in the refrigerator. Make sure to remove any spoiled ones to prevent others from going bad.
